Biography

Stephen loves the challenges that come with working with data and the collaborations that feed into data discovery. As a solutions architect, Stephen has been involved in designing and implementing cloud-based solutions across a variety of industries, including healthcare, finance, and public health. His key interests currently involve designing and building solutions using AI/ML tooling and improving the search experience with hybrid search.
